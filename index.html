Vincent Busch
Game Designer / Developer

Welcome to my portfolio website! My name is Vincent and I make games. After finishing my Game Design studies in 2023 I have developed and published my strategy game Konkwest, and while I keep supporting the game with patches and content, I am looking for the next step in my career.

Games and computer technology have been my passion for as long as I can remember. It started when I collected and hand-crafted trading cards in school, and I have therefore styled this website in reference to my fondness of card games.
