Volt Merchandise

Since 2021 I have taken on the role of Merchandise Lead at Volt Europa. As part of that, I have designed print graphics for various merchandise items, such as the t-shirt and the backpack shown off by Volt member Fabian in the picture.

Overall I have made print designs for lots of clothing items, cups, backpacks and pillows.

The designs make use of the graphics and design guidelines set up with Volt Europa's design team and the Hamburg-based design agency Karl Anders.

International Discussion Group Groningen

In order to help formalizing and expanding my friends' discussion group, I designed a logo, a series of promotional graphics following a template and the website of the IDGG.

The group consists of international students in Groningen, meeting to share in discuss politics and news, and occassionally talking to politicians as well.

Volt Social Media Content

I learnt to use the collaborative design tool Figma and applied Volt's brand guidelines to create social media content for Volt.

The example image is a graphic made for Instagram, promoting an AMA event with Volt Europa's president Reinier van Lansschot in 2020.
