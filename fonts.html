Fonts

Novarley

A futuristic font, inspired by sci-fi and US college style aesthetics.

Made in Groningen, 2020 - 2021.

Vindicator

An sharp and rounded font with no point on its i.

Made in Groningen, 2019.

Narrovind

A narrow font combining (neo-)gothic inspirations with angular and simplistic influences. It features Latin and Cyrillic characters.

Made in Groningen, 2019.

Saiberfouk

A blocky, somewhat futuristic, mostly sans-serif font. All latin characters are in square dimensions. Lower case letters are a third of the size of upper case letters.

Made in Groningen, 2020.

VCTekst

A rounded simplistic monospace font with digital-looking numbers and some special characters made for use with English or German language.

Made in Hamburg, 2018.

PikselRîformd

This pixel-art-style font supports almost all latin characters, many greek characters and the capital cyrillic characters.

Made in Hamburg, 2018.
