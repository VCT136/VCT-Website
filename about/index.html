About Me

I am Vincent. As a student of Communication & Multimedia Design majoring in Game Design, I have developed skills in Game Design, Project Management, Programming & Visual Design.

I see an interesting variety of tasks in the field of creative media and design, and enjoy many of its awesome products. I love the achievement of putting things together and seeing them work. It makes me happy to see people enjoy what I made. Critical thinking, initiative, a focus on efficiency and team leadership are skills that contribute to my competence.

Working on digital product / game design projects in my study programme, I have found to enjoy doing game design and team / project management, as well as programming work.

My favorite video games, of which most are situated in the genres of strategy and action games, frequently inspire me.

Apart from games, my interests lie in the fields of history, politics, geography, linguistics and arts.
As a member of student organization SIB (Dutch United Nations Student Association) I can follow many interesting lectures and events on such topics. Additionally, I am an active member of the first pan-European political party Volt, for which I manage the official merchandise shop and design materials.
