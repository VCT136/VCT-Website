List of Radical Sans-Serif Fonts

Over the course of the past seveal years I have developed a strong affection for radical sans-serif typefaces. By that I mean ones which aren't just without serifs, but even remove (nearly) all the useless, small terminals, swashes and strokes, which most sans-serif ones maintain, from the letters.

Because I haven't seen a comprehensive list of such fonts anywhere, I decided to make a list of my own. That list is what you see below.
I decided only to include typefaces which support both upper and lower case latin letters as a criterion.
